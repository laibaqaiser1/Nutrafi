## ğŸ“… Meal Plans
**Features:**
- **View all meal plans** - See all customer meal plans with status
- **Create meal plan** - 4-step process:
  1. Select customer
  2. Choose plan (predefined weekly/monthly or custom)
  3. Add payment info
  4. Configure meals - assign dishes to each day/time slot
- **View meal plan** - See full schedule:
  - Weekly/monthly calendar view
  - Each meal shows dish, calories, status (Active/Skipped/Delivered)
  - Daily and weekly totals

- **Edit meal plan** - Update plan details
- **Add dishes to skipped meals** - Click skipped meal to add dish later
- **Mark as delivered** - Track which meals are delivered
